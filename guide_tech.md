# Faith Lock & Safe | Job Management System
## Tech Guide (Milton, Zach, Thomas)

This guide covers everything you do in the system. Open your bookmarked URL in your phone's browser. Sign in once. Your phone should keep you signed in. If it logs you out, use the tech email and password Rich gave you.

No app to download. No account to create. Just the browser.

---

### Your Responsibilities in the System

You see your assigned jobs, complete the job form after finishing work, log parts and labor, record payment, upload photos if needed, and flag anything Rich needs to know about. You do not see pricing. You do not touch other techs' jobs unless you are assigned to them.

---

## Step 1: Seeing Your Jobs

When you sign in you land on **My Jobs**. It defaults to today's jobs.

Three filter buttons at the top:
- **Today** - jobs assigned for today (default)
- **This Week** - all your jobs this week
- **All Open** - everything assigned to you that is not yet approved or cancelled

Each job shows as a card with the account name, date, job type, and status. If Rich added scope or site notes, they appear on the card.

**Red banner** means the job is fixed price. Rich quoted the customer a specific amount. Do not add work or parts beyond the original scope without calling Rich first.

**Blue banner** means Rich pre-loaded expected parts and labor. These are already entered on your completion form. Review them when you open the form and adjust if the job went differently than planned.

---

## Step 2: Completing a Job

When the job is done, tap **Complete Job** on the job card.

Fill out the completion form:

**Time In / Time Out** - when you arrived and when you left. Use the date/time picker. This feeds into billing.

**Parts Used** - type any part of the name in the search box. Results appear as you type. Tap a part to add it. You will be asked for the quantity. Parts are pre-loaded if Rich entered expected materials. Add, remove, or adjust quantities as needed to reflect what you actually used.

**Labor** - select the labor type from the dropdown, enter hours, tap Add. If Rich pre-loaded labor, it appears in the table. Adjust the hours to match actual time on site.

**Payment Type** - Cash, Check, Card, or Invoice. Select whichever applies.
- Cash: enter the amount collected in the detail field
- Check: enter the check number
- Card: note card type or last four digits if you have them
- Invoice: leave detail blank or note any reference number

**Tech Notes** - anything Rich needs to know. Unexpected conditions, customer requests, issues you ran into, reasons a job is incomplete. Write it here. This goes directly to Rich's review queue.

**Flag for Follow-up** - check this box if the job is not finished, if you need Rich to contact the customer, or if anything is unresolved. This puts a flag on the record in Rich's queue.

**Photos** - tap the file input to upload photos from your phone's camera roll or take new ones. Photos are stored under the account and are visible to Rich in the Accounts page. Resize happens automatically.

**Quote Form** - only fill this out if Rich sent you for a site survey and you need to document scope and materials for a quote. Leave it blank for standard jobs.

Tap **Submit Completion**. The job moves to Rich's Pending Review queue immediately.

---

## Step 3: If You Need to Undo a Submission

If you submitted by mistake or need to correct something and Rich has not yet reviewed it, tap **Unsubmit** on the job card. This removes your submission and returns the job to Scheduled. You can then re-open it and submit again correctly.

Do not unsubmit if you are not sure whether Rich has already reviewed it. If in doubt, call Rich and have him use Reopen / Edit on his end instead.

---

## Step 4: Adding a Note to a Past Job

For jobs submitted in the last two weeks, you can add a timestamped note without reopening the whole form. Tap **Add Note** on the job card (visible after submission) and type your note. It is appended to your tech notes with the date and time. Use this for follow-up information that comes in after you leave the site.

---

## Step 5: Logging an Unscheduled Job

If you take a call in the field that was not on the schedule, tap **+ New Job** at the top.

Fill in the account, job type, date, and scope. Then complete the rest of the form exactly like a standard completion. When you submit, the job goes directly to Rich's Pending Review queue. He will review and approve it like any other job.

If the customer is not in the system, use **+ New Customer** instead to submit their details. Rich will add them to the account list. In the meantime, do your best to select the closest matching account or leave it blank and explain in tech notes.

---

## Step 6: Submitting a New Customer

Tap **+ New Customer** at the top.

Enter the customer's name, address, phone, and any notes. Tap Submit. This sends the record to Rich who will add them to the permanent account list. You do not need to wait for this before logging your job.

---

## Things to Remember

- You never see pricing. That is intentional
- The red banner is a hard stop. Call Rich before doing work beyond the quoted scope
- Payment detail matters. A check number or cash amount saves Brenda time when she keys invoices
- If the job went differently than the scope described, put it in tech notes
- If something is wrong at the site (access problem, wrong address, customer not there), call Rich and note it in tech notes when you submit
- The follow-up flag is for Rich, not for you to resolve. Check it and move on
- If you are not the lead tech on a multi-tech job, you cannot submit the completion form. The lead tech submits for the whole job
